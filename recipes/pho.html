Pho

Pho is a traditional Vietnamese soup consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat, typically beef or chicken. The flavorful broth, simmered for hours with spices like star anise, cloves, and cinnamon, forms the base of the dish. Fresh garnishes such as bean sprouts, basil, lime, and chili peppers are added to taste, creating a balanced, aromatic, and hearty meal that is enjoyed as a staple in Vietnamese cuisine.

  • Beef bones/chicken bones
  • Sugar and Salt
  • Rice noodles
  • Beef
  • Bean sprouts
  • Cilantro
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Sriracha sauce
